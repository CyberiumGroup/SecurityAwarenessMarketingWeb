**Case Study**

TechBridge Solutions — Turning Compliance into Culture

TechBridge, a 120-person financial services firm, was struggling with the typical security awareness problem: annual compliance training that employees dreaded, clicked through as fast as possible, and forgot within days. Their phishing simulation click rates were sitting at around 34%, well above the industry benchmark.

They adopted a gamified security awareness platform in place of their legacy LMS-based training. The platform used short scenario-based challenges, leaderboards, team competitions, and a points system tied to real incentives like gift cards and extra time off. Training modules ran 3–5 minutes rather than 60, and were delivered monthly rather than annually.

After 12 months the results were significant. Phishing click rates dropped to 8%. Incident reporting by employees — one of the hardest behaviours to shift — increased by 60%, meaning the security team had much earlier visibility into threats. Overall training completion rates went from around 55% to 94%, largely because employees were engaging voluntarily rather than just meeting a deadline.

Perhaps more importantly, the CISO was able to demonstrate measurable cultural change to the board, which helped secure budget for broader security investment the following year.
